# Pâte à choux
## Ingredients
* 60 g milk
* 16 g butter
* small pinch salt
* 30 g ap flour
* 1 large egg
## Ingredients X 2
* 120 g milk
* 32 g butter
* medium pinch salt
* 60 g ap flour
* 2 large eggs
## Ingredients X 3
* 180 grams milk
* 50 g butter
* pinch of salt
* 90 grams ap flour
* 3 large eggs


last egg might be too large, we can beat the egg and add it little by little to get wanted consistency of dough
