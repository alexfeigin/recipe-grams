110 g flour
35 g olive oil
40 g water
3 g salt

mix flour and oil until sandy flour. (about 1 min)
add luke warm water (30 deg) and salt to flour and mix well.
kneed dough for 4-5 minutes. rest dough for 30 minutes.
make 30g dough balls. press into spheres with taco press.
cook on medium heat grittle around 20 seconds for the first side and 10 seconds on the second.
