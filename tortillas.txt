120 g flour
30 g olive oil
60 g water
3 g salt

mix flour and oil until sandy flour. (about 1 min)
add luke warm water (30 deg) and salt to flour and mix well.
kneed dough for 4-5 minutes. rest dough for 30 minutes.
make 35g dough balls. press into cicles with taco press.
cook on medium heat griddle around 90 seconds for the first side and 30 seconds on the second.
