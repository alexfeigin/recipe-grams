# Gluten-Free Chocolate Banana Brownies

## Ingredients:
- 2 ripe bananas
- 5 g baking soda
- 120 g almond butter
- 1 egg
- 60 g maple syrup
- 1 g salt
- 50 g chocolate chips
- 32 g cocoa powder

## Instructions:
1. Mash the bananas in a bowl.
2. Add all other ingredients *except* the chocolate chips and mix until smooth.
3. Fold in the chocolate chips.
4. Pour the batter into a baking pan lined with parchment paper, *20×20 cm*.
5. Bake at *180°C* for *30 minutes*.
