# Vegan Vanilla Sponge Cake
## Ingredients (grams):

* 180g ap flour
* 200g granulated sugar
* 3g salt
* 6g baking soda
* 5g white vinegar
* 70g canola oil
* 6g vanilla extract
* 240 grams water

Preheat oven to 180c fan on.
Mix dry separately, add wet and mix until combined
bake for 30 min or until dry (internal temp 98c)
